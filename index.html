Our Story

A2Z is a gaming and coffee haven. Our cafe offers a variety of coffee and tea options made by expert baristas, and a range of games including Playstation consoles, board games, and card games. We're committed to providing a positive experience and making a positive impact in our community. Come join us for your next caffeine and controller fix!

Why We Are Special?

  • Coffee and drinks made with high-quality beans and ingredients!
  • Playstation consoles, board games, and card games for guests to enjoy.
  • Welcoming and inclusive space with plenty of seating options.
  • Knowledgeable staff offering recommendations and guidance to enhance your experience.

Our Location

Our cafe and Playstation lounge is located in Sheraton, El Montaza right next to the El Sanhoury Automotive Dealership, making it easy to stop by for a quick coffee or a gaming session with friends. We offer a variety of specialty coffee and tea drinks, as well as freshly made pastries, sandwiches, and salads. With plenty of comfortable seating options available, you can relax and enjoy your coffee or game in our cozy and welcoming space. We are open 7 days a week, so you can visit us anytime that works for you. Whether you're looking to catch up with friends, get some work done, or take a break from your day and play your favorite games, we're here to serve you.
